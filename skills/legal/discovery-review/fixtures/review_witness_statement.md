## TEST VALLEY RAILROAD — WITNESS STATEMENT

**Incident Number:** IR-2024-11472
**Witness:** B.C., Switchman
**Employee ID:** TVRR-E-05182
**Date of Statement:** November 14, 2024
**Statement Taken By:** TVRR Safety Department

**Statement:**

I, B.C., was working as Switchman on the Extra Board crew on the evening of November 12, 2024. We were assigned to make a pick-up of a standing cut of cars on Track 2 at Northgate Yard. The cut had been left there by the afternoon yard crew. Our crew consisted of Engineer L.M., Conductor J.T., myself as switchman, and Trainee S.N.

When we arrived at Track 2, I walked the cut to check the cars and make the coupling. I noticed that handbrakes were not applied on the standing cut. I radioed this to Conductor J.T., who acknowledged and said he would report it. J.T. then began aligning the switch to enter Track 2.

Engineer L.M. coupled the locomotive to the standing cut. When the locomotive made contact, the standing cars moved instead of coupling. They rolled approximately 6-8 feet. I was about 40 feet away on the opposite side of the track. I saw Conductor J.T. near the switch stand get struck by the moving car and thrown backwards into the switch stand. I yelled a warning but it happened too fast for him to react. He went down hard on his left side and did not get up.

I ran to J.T. and found him conscious but in obvious pain. He was holding his left shoulder and saying his ribs and hip hurt. He also said his head hurt. Engineer L.M. called for EMS on the radio. The ambulance arrived in about 20 minutes and took J.T. to the hospital.

I have worked at Northgate Yard for about 4 years. The lighting on Track 2 has always been dim — you need a lantern even when the yard lights are on. I recall at least one prior incident where cars were not properly secured on Track 2. I think it was sometime in summer 2024. There was no injury that time, but it was a close call. I believe D.W.'s crew was involved in that incident too.

Our crew size was 4 that night, which is the minimum allowed now. Before May of this year, we usually had 5 on switching jobs. It feels like we're short-handed, especially at night.

I have read this statement and confirm it is true and correct to the best of my knowledge.

/s/ B.C.

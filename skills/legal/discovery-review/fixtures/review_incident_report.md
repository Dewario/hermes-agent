## TEST VALLEY RAILROAD — INCIDENT REPORT

**Incident Number:** IR-2024-11472
**Date of Incident:** November 12, 2024
**Time:** 22:30 hours (approximate)
**Location:** Northgate Yard, Milepost 47.3, Main Line Track 2
**Division:** North District

**Employees Involved:**
- J.T., Freight Conductor (injured)
- L.M., Engineer
- B.C., Switchman
- S.N., Conductor Trainee

**Equipment Involved:**
- Locomotive TVRR #4721 (GP38-2), last inspection 2024-11-01 (passed)
- 47-car mixed freight consist
- Standing cut of 12 cars on Track 2 (cars TVRR 302184, TVRR 401762, etc.)

**Description of Incident:**

At approximately 22:30 hours on November 12, 2024, Extra Board crew operating Locomotive TVRR #4721 was performing switching operations at Northgate Yard, Milepost 47.3, Track 2. Crew was making a pick-up of a standing cut of 12 cars that had been placed on Track 2 by the earlier yard crew (Yard Foreman D.W., off duty at time of incident).

Conductor J.T. was on the ground aligning Switch #NP-12 for the movement. Engineer L.M. coupled Locomotive TVRR #4721 to the standing cut. Upon coupling, the standing cut of cars moved approximately 6-8 feet eastward instead of coupling securely. Conductor J.T. was positioned near Switch #NP-12, approximately 15 feet from the point of impact. The car adjacent to Conductor J.T.'s position (TVRR 401762) moved during the roll, and its step struck Conductor J.T. on the left side. Conductor J.T. was thrown into the switch stand.

Engineer L.M. immediately stopped the movement and radioed for emergency assistance. Local EMS arrived at 22:52 and transported Conductor J.T. to County General Hospital.

**Initial Findings:**

- Standing cut of 12 cars on Track 2 did not have handbrakes applied. Yard Foreman D.W.'s crew had placed the cut approximately 18:00 hours and departed without setting handbrakes, in violation of GCOR Rule 5.8 (Securement of Equipment Left Unattended).
- Track 2 at this location has a slight descending grade (approximately 0.3%) eastward.
- Yard lighting on Track 2 was noted as functional but at minimum illumination level per TVRR Standard YP-7. No lighting deficiency was noted during the post-incident inspection.
- Locomotive TVRR #4721 passed its most recent inspection on 2024-11-01 with no defects noted.
- No alcohol or drug involvement suspected. Post-incident testing per FRA regulations completed; results pending.

**Corrective Action:**

- Yard Foreman D.W. suspended pending formal investigation.
- Safety bulletin to be issued re: GCOR Rule 5.8 compliance.
- Review of yard lighting levels scheduled.

**Prior Incidents at Location:**

A search of incident records for Northgate Yard, Milepost 47.3, Track 2, for the period January 2023 through November 2024 returned one prior incident: IR-2024-08941 (August 8, 2024), involving a similar near-miss with an inadequately secured cut on Track 2. No injuries in the prior incident. Corrective action from that incident included a safety briefing for yard crews on GCOR Rule 5.8.

# Jurisdiction Source Packet

**Source checked:** 2026-07-20
**Scope:** plaintiff-side civil discovery review/drafting support and expert-needs
triage for California trial courts, San Bernardino Superior Court, Washington
superior courts, King County Superior Court, and Pierce County Superior Court.

This packet records official source locations used to populate the local rule
packs. It is a retrieval trail, not owner authorization and not a substitute for
case-specific legal research, department orders, standing orders, or docket
deadlines.

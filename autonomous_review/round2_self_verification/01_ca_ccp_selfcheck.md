## Findings

- **No drift.** All ten cites confirm against the official leginfo text as of 2026-07-20.
- **One nuance to record (not an error):** § 801.1's operative Jan 1 2024 version is the product of **two** bills — SB 652 (added) + SB 135 (scoped to "general civil case"). The skill/handoff cite "SB 652" is correct but incomplete; a future correction could note "added by SB 652, scoped by SB 135, operative Jan 1 2024." This does not change any resolver logic.
- **AB 1521 (eff. Jan 1 2026) is real and load-bearing** for the UD timing (5→10 days across §§ 2030.020/2031.020/2033.020) and the meet-and-confer/abuse definitions (§§ 2016.040/2023.010). Confirmed against Ch. 200 of the 2025 Statutes.
- **SB 235 (eff. Jan 1 2024)** is real for the § 2023.050 $1,000 sanction. Confirmed against Ch. 284 of the 2023 Statutes.
